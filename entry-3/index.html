as october rolls around, a constant petrichor whiffs in the air and my stomach craves spicy soup. i feel like it’s a science? sichuan spiced broth, infinite noodles, fresh bokchoy, meat and tofu - there’s no way you’re not craving it as you read! well if you want, head over to YGF Malatang on 3rd Ave and thank me later. I started off a gloomy Sunday almost staining my favorite sweater, but it was worth it.

5th ave is so surreal!

Sunday + rain = me shopping for a puffer for the season. There I was, a long way from home (I’m being dramatic, it was like 25 blocks), surrounded with more things to buy than my pockets could afford. Naturally, I was lost, trying to find my way to UNIQLO, when I came across this water display. When I photographed it, it looked like a pool of gold, glistening down all sparkly. I love sparkles.

what are you wearing?

an iced latte a day, keeps me awake

Rain on the sidewalk, umbrella dripping, and I still order it iced. Cold glass, clink of cubes, oat milk turning the espresso a soft caramel. I take the first sip and my brain finally switches on. The window fogs a little, people float by in shiny coats, and I start scribbling notes I did not know I had in me. If you want to make it extra good, add a tiny pinch of cardamom and cinnamon. The corner café on 3rd Ave feels like a study nook today, just the right kind of hum. I leave with chilly hands and a warm mind. Perfect trade.
